# Lexicardio Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Lexicardio is provided by individual developer 安東尼Andongni (referred to as "the App", "we", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

## 1. Accounts and Personal Information

1. The App does not provide any account or sign-in functionality.
2. We do not create or store any user accounts, passwords, or personally identifiable information (PII) on any server.

## 2. Data Storage and Cloud Services

1. The App does not offer cloud synchronization.
2. All learning-related data is stored locally on the user's device or in a folder explicitly chosen by the user and is not automatically uploaded to any remote server.

## 3. Advertising and Tracking

1. The App does not display any third-party advertisements.
2. The App does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or behavioral tracking SDKs (such as ad identifiers or cross-app tracking).

## 4. File Read/Write Permissions

1. To allow users to import or export vocabulary data, the App may request permission to read from or write to user-selected files or folders.
2. The App only accesses locations explicitly chosen by the user and only reads or writes files that are related to Lexicardio's functionality (such as vocabulary lists).
3. The App does not access unrelated files or folders without the user's consent.

## 5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

1. The App does not share user data with any third-party services or companies.
2. The App currently does not integrate third-party analytics or cloud services that require uploading personal data.

## 6. Children's Privacy

1. The App is not specifically designed for children under the age of 13.
2. Since the App does not collect personally identifiable information, we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 7. Device and Technical Information

1. The App may store necessary error information locally on the device (for example, crash information) to help improve stability and fix bugs.
2. This information is stored only on the user's device and is not automatically transmitted to the developer. Users may choose to share such information manually with the developer when requesting technical support.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

1. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time due to feature changes or legal requirements.
2. In case of significant changes, we will describe them in the updated version of the App. We recommend that users review this page periodically.
